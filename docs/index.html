Everything you need to monitor and maintain optimal hive health.

Instant Mite Detection

Our proprietary algorithm detects reddish Varroa mites using precise color thresholding, saturation checks, and Hough Circle Transforms—even in complex backgrounds.

📊

Smart Recommendations

Based on Honey Bee Health Coalition guidelines, our engine evaluates mite ratios against queen age and bee breed to provide accurate, actionable treatment advice.

🛡️

Proactive Protection

Identify critical thresholds (e.g., >3% infestation) before winter sets in. Early detection significantly increases colony survival odds.
